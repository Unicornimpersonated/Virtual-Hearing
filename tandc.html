CLIENT PRE-TRIAL CONFERENCE ETIQUETTE DECLARATION FORM


I hereby acknowledge and agree to adhere to the following rules and etiquettes during the pre-trial conference to ensure a respectful and professional atmosphere. I affirm my commitment to these guidelines.


1. Dress Code:


I understand and acknowledge that I shall attire myself in a respectful and appropriate manner for the pre-trial conference. I will adhere to a dress code that encompasses clean, well-fitting clothing, and I will refrain from wearing overly casual attire.


2. Punctuality:


I acknowledge the critical importance of punctuality. I shall attend the video conference promptly and demonstrate my respect for the court, opposing parties, and my legal representatives by arriving on time.


3. Video Conference Setup:


I will ensure that my video conferencing setup is meticulously prepared. This includes having a reliable internet connection, optimal lighting, and a clutter-free, professional background. I shall rigorously test my video and audio equipment in advance to pre-empt technical disruptions.


4. Speaking Politely:


I shall address the presiding Judge, opposing counsel, and all other participants with unfaltering respect and courtesy. I shall avoid any form of interruption and patiently await my designated opportunity to speak.


5. Following Instructions


I hereby pledge to comply with any directives or guidance issued by the presiding Judge throughout the conference. I understand that such instructions may encompass specific procedures for evidence presentation, settlement discussions, or dispute resolution.


6. Confidentiality:


I fully comprehend and respect the imperative of maintaining the confidentiality of the pre-trial conference. I shall refrain from divulging any details of the proceedings to individuals who are not directly involved in the case.


7. Emotions and Outbursts:


I pledge to maintain a composed demeanour and to eschew any form of emotional outburst during the conference. I am acutely aware that impassioned arguments or emotional displays could detrimentally impact the proceedings.


I affirm my commitment to uphold these rules and etiquette during the pre-trial conference, with a clear recognition that any breach may make me liable to be punished by the presiding Judge or relevant authorities.
